## Keyword Cannibalization Audit
- **Issue**: `/tours` and `/from/ranchi` could compete for "Tour Packages from Ranchi".
- **Resolution**: `/from/ranchi` is kept NOINDEX to prevent cannibalization and doorway penalties. The primary URL for this intent remains `/tours` and the homepage `/`.
- **Issue**: `/dharmic-yatra` vs individual spiritual tours.
- **Resolution**: Category page targets broad intent ("Pilgrimage Tour Packages"), while individual tour pages target specific destinations (e.g., "Char Dham Yatra").
